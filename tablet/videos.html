Is my Animation I final project. The story behind the video is that man kind has destroyed the Earth's atmosphere through  the burning of fossil fuels. As a result, Oceans have become more rough and lightning continues to strike various spots on the Earth. The World is in a constant storm, and it is always night. Man kind has gone extinct as a result and Aliens have come to investigate.

This is a music video I made for my Video Editing course. The footage was taken from the videogame  Castlevania Lords of Shadow. The story revolves around Gabriel Belmont and his fight to save humanity from Monsters such as Werewolves Vampires and more.

Is a mini game I programed with javascript for my intro to programming course. The game generates gray scale colored squares. It is the players job to avoid letting the screen become filled with gray scale colored squares by pressing the appropriate keys to generate multi colored squares. The player has options for which color he or she wants to paint the canvas with.
