About Me:


Hello, Welcome to my portfolio website which I hand coded myself. My name is Tony Smith and I am an Interactive Multimedia Major with a minor in Fine art. My aspiration is to become a Videogame Artist (primarily one who focuses on the landscapes and architecture within videogames). My interests include drawing, 3D modeling, game design, and videogames themselves. On this website you can see some of the work I have done through out my academic career, and during my spare time.
